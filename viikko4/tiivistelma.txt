Start, Stop, Continue, More of, Less of Wheel is a short meta discussion on moving forward. It allows open-ended feedback in a team. A circle is drawn on a paper and divided into 5 sections, and asking what should we start doing, what should we stop, what should we continue, what should we do more of and what should we do less of. There are also bigger variations for bigger groups.

Glad, Sad, Mad can take longer, but isn't supposed to go deeper. Issues are categorized into groups by if they made participants glad, sad or mad. They are written on papers anonymously, and afterwards are taken one by one. Everyone marks cards by if they want to discuss them,  and the chair then leads the conversation
